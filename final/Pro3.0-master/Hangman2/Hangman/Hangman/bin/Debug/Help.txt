How to Register:

Click the register button, add your username and clcik enter. 
If your chosen username already exists you will be prompted to give another.


How to Play:

Once you have been registered you can sign in by entering your username and clicking play
Then go on to choosing your category.
Once you have chosen your category you will be redrected to the game window
The aim of the game is to figure out as many words as you can under the time frame without killing the man on the screen
Thus you get five chances to make a mistake throughout the game 
To submit a word simply click the 'Enter' key
